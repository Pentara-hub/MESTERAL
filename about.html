About Mesteral

Shaping the Future of Pharmaceuticals and Industrial Excellence

Who We Are

Mesteral for Developed Industries L.L.C is a pharmaceutical and industrial company headquartered in Egypt, with global reach through partnerships in the UAE, Bulgaria, and Azerbaijan.

  • Trusted by governments across MENA
  • Advanced production and packaging lines
  • Collaborative R&D with international partners

Our Vision

To become a cornerstone in the advancement of pharmaceutical manufacturing and global industrial collaboration.

Our Mission

To empower healthcare innovation by developing scalable, ethical, and globally integrated production systems.
