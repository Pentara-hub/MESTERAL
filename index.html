Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Mesteral services, partnerships, and regional operations across the MENA, Africa, and CIS regions.

What does Mesteral specialize in?

Mesteral specializes in business integration for international pharmaceutical and medical suppliers. We help companies enter and expand in the MENA, Africa, and CIS markets through exclusive distribution agreements and strategic support.

Do you assist with local product registration and marketing?

Yes, we provide full support in registering products with local authorities, managing dealer networks, and launching marketing campaigns. We ensure regulatory compliance and successful market entry for all our partners.

What regions does Mesteral operate in?

We operate across the Middle East, North and Central Africa, and CIS/Eastern Europe. Our offices in Egypt, UAE, Bulgaria, and Azerbaijan help us maintain strong local networks and provide hands-on market support.
